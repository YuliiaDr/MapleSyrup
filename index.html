I love Maple Syrup

A natural healthy sweetener

Maple syrup is made from the rising spring sap of a maple tree native to the US and Canada. It is reduced by boiling in open pan and when finished it is about 66 per cent sucrose and has a honey-like flavour. Although maple syrup is a sweetener, and therefore should be limited in the diet, it may contribute to better health in a few unique ways.

  • Gets a Lower Score on the Glycemic Index
  • May Help Protect Against Cancer
  • Helps Protect Skin Health
  • Healthier Alternative to Sugar
  • Supplies Important Vitamins and Minerals
  • May Enhance Antibiotic Effects
